About Our Ministries

Saints Harvest Church is a Community of believers fulfilling the great commission according to Mathew 28:16-20: ‘all authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore, go ye and make disciples of all Nations, baptising them in the name of the Father, Son and the Holy Spirit and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age’. Jesus said ‘the harvest is plentiful but the workers are few. Ask the Lord of the harvest, therefore to send out workers into His harvest field’ Luke 10: 2.
